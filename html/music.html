Some Songs I've Written

Mostly different genres of electronic music right now.

Please Stand By!

Whether it's technical difficulties or just time for a break, this lo-fi song is fit for winding down after some intensity.

Stutter

A more active song, fit for getting in the zone.

Air-Conditioned

There's nothing like being in an air-conditioned space, and this song's "shopping mall"-like instrumentation is sure to elicit that feeling.

Piston

This song starts off mysterious and "enclosed", but each drop launches you out into the open, before you fall back in and the space closes around you again.

Now For the Weather

This was just a fun spoof of one of the news channel's weather display songs, but this one's a lot more involved.

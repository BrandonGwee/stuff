About Me

I'm a student of Singapore Polytechnic studying for a diploma in Information Technology (programming, app design, working with other people, data analysis and storytelling). I also have quite a number of hobbies, my efforts and ideas being showcased on this website, and I'm always eager to meet new people whom I can collaborate with.

I had also wanted to be a content creator since childhood, which resulted in me sometimes acting out scenes I have in my imagination (i.e. talking to nobody), but it's probably okay if you express curiosity about what I'm saying. And I still would like to be a part of social media, but now my attitude has changed and I would rather be a host or an editor - some part of production - on a larger team - working with friends.

Who is This Dog That Appears On All My Socials?

This is Khroma, the identity I use online in order to maintain anonymity. In the same way most people identify their username or avatar as themself, I also intended Khroma to just be "me as an anonymous internet user, or as a fictional character if I'm not acting in person", rather than his own character that is completely separated from me. As such, I'm having a hard time deciding whether to refer to Khroma in the first person, or the third person. (For now, he will be referred to with third person pronouns)

Important traits of the character
  • Khroma's appearance and anatomy is based off of dogs (I'm Singaporean Chinese, it's my zodiac), and lion dancers, so he literally has 4 legs (whereas real-world dogs' forelimbs are more like human hands than human legs), and his chest is as far from the top of his head as his waist is from his chest.
  • He can change the colour of his fur and "eyes", but he has to guess where the colours go.
  • His "eyes" are actually lenses like goggles, and some extreme expressions may reveal his/my real eyes behind them. Though he does use the lenses like eyes, the "white shine" being his pupils.
  • Personality wise, he is just me and his mannerisms and motivations are the same as mine.
  • Khroma's fur is prehensile, and he likes to change his fur-style along with his colour. He might also use the fur on his belly as hands if he really needs them, but he prefers to use his upper legs first.
